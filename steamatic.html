ALMIRA STEAMATIC

Home Services Gallary

Al Mira Steamatic is a master franchisee of Steamatic Inc
USA. Our company provides high quality indoor air
services and emergency response services. 

Al Mira Steamatic continues to penetrate the market by focusing on three types of customers; the home owners, the building owners/managers and the insurance adjuster

We provide them all the necessary services to keep the indoor environment clean and recover from fires, flood and natural disaster at the lowest costs in the quickest possible time frame

Al Mira Investment Group Co. Ltd established Al Mira Steamatic in 1991 currently operating in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia with offices located in Dammam, Jubail, Jeddah, Makkah, Madina, Yanbu and Riyadh, we recently opened our branch in UAE Dubai

 Al Mira Steamatic has a history of being a leader in the indoor air quality and emergency response business. We have eleven profit sources namely Fire Restoration, Water Restoration, Carpet Cleaning, Furniture Cleaning, Drapery Cleaning, Air Duct and Coil Cleaning, Wood Restoration, Ceiling Cleaning and Wall and Floor Cleaning

We are proud to announce that Al Mira Steamatic is certified and accredited by some of the biggest companies and institutes in Restoration service
